Bluetooth Core Specification 4.2 is an important update to the Bluetooth Core Specification delivering exciting new features and benefits for Bluetooth Smart.

Bluetooth® Core Specification 4.2

This will create significant advantages for developers and manufacturers, while providing a better user experience for their customers. Bluetooth 4.2 makes Bluetooth Smart even smarter, faster and the ideal wireless technology for the Internet of Things (IoT).
